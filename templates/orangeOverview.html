Orange Twitter Analysis Prototypes

Month View/Overview

The time series chart at the left shows volume of tweets and selects ranges of time. To zoom into a range of time, select/highlight it with the mouse or pinch if you are using a multi-touch device (to unzoom, double click or unpinch). The pie graph in the middle will be updated to reflect the sentiment of the time range you select. The sections of the pie can be selected to show the corresponding tweets in the table below. The slider at the right allows you to select subsets of the tweets based on valence ranges. For example, to select positive tweets, move the lower slider up, or to select neutral tweets, move the upper slider down and the lower slider up so that the range enclosed the midpoint of the bar. Underneath, you can see some of the tweets used to generate the charts. Right now, the slider, pie chart, and data table are connected so that the view of these will be updated to reflect the current selection. The time-series is intended to do the same, but currently there is the possibility that too much data will be returned and slow down the browser.
